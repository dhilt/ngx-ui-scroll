The datasource could be limited. In this sample we have only 1000 items in the dataset: from -99 to 900 including 0. If at some point the uiScroll will request this datasource for, say, 10 items started from -105 index, then the result will consist of only 4 items: -99, -98, -97, -96. It is also important to return empty array if we are out of range.

Another option of how to limit the datasource is to use minIndex and maxIndex settings; this way the implementation of the Datasource.get method could remain unlimited as the uiScroll will not request for items that are out of [minIndex; maxIndex] range.
